FruitSalad is an easy to use way to check what certificates your food has got. With it, you can find out the history of your food product. Each ingredient is tracked on the blockchain from farmer to the store, with a constant check of all food quality certifications such as free of nuts and Halal. Because we use blockchain to track ownership of each ingredient, you can be guaranteed of their history and be confident that their certifications are true.
